Gamified Sleep Experience
Oct 2021, Research Work

Nighttime phone use is a major cause of sleep disorders. Our approach is to introduce lightweight interventions across the sleep cycle to gently modify this behavior. The core of this strategy is a breath-controlled audio game that reframes the smartphone as a sleep aid. It uses gamification to guide users through breathing exercises, turning the source of the problem into the solution for a healthier sleep habit.
